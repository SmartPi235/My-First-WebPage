<p>What was the first name of Minecraft?
Pre-classic is the name given to the prototype versions developed before Classic. It was the very first development phase in Minecraft, lasting for a week.
 The phase was originally named "Cave Game" until the game was changed to "Minecraft: Order of the Stone" then to "Minecraft".</p>

<p>Is Minecraft educational?
Yes, Minecraft is educational because it enhances creativity, problem-solving, self-direction, collaboration, and other life skills.

<p>What is Minecraft coded in?
